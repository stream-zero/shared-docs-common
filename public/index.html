Our Products

STREAMZERO FX is our flagship platform designed to accelerate the creation of Python based event-driven microservices.

STREAMZERO K8X(Alpha) is our platform designed to enable event driven Kubernetes - accelerate the creation of polyglot event-driven microservices.

AML

In the realm of financial compliance and risk management, the integration of event-driven microservices offers a powerful approach to enhancing anti-money laundering(AML) capabilities.
